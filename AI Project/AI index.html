AI Recipe App

Welcome to the AI Recipe App! This application uses artificial intelligence to help you find and create delicious recipes based on your preferences.

Features:

  • Search for recipes by ingredients, cuisine, or dietary restrictions.
  • Get personalized recipe recommendations based on your taste preferences.
  • Create and save your own recipes with AI-generated suggestions.
  • Access nutritional information for each recipe.

Getting Started:

To start using the AI Recipe App, simply enter your ingredients or preferences in the search bar and let the AI do the rest!

Enjoy cooking with the help of AI!
