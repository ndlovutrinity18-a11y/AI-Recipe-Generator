AI Recipe Website

Welcome to the AI Recipe Website! This application uses artificial intelligence to help you find and create delicious recipes based on your preferences.

eg: Chicken Curry, Beef Stew, Vegan Tacos

Features:

  • Search for recipes by ingredients, cuisine, or dietary restrictions.
  • Get personalized recipe recommendations based on your taste preferences.
  • Create and save your own recipes with AI-generated suggestions.
  • Access nutritional information for each recipe.
